History

The publishing house "Veselin Masleša" from Sarajevo, is one of the most prestigious publishing houses in the former Yugoslavia, was founded on June 10, 1950. Today, our publishing house is called Sarajevo Publishing. Its recognizability and its social significance have not changed with the change of name. Sarajevo Publishing has remained the initiator and holder of numerous national projects in Bosnia and Herzegovina, from textbooks, professional, lexicographic and cultural books and manuals, to fine literature, children's books and other valuable publications. Generations of Bosnian intelligentsia, scientists and experts of all profiles have been brought up on nearly seven thousand editions, which our house has published so far, through a dozen libraries, which have become a sign of recognition and a mark of past times in this segment. It is enough to mention projects such as libraries Cultural Heritage of Bosnia and Herzegovina, Logos, Lastavica, Ars, and textbooks and manuals for primary and secondary schools and universities.Our responsibility is growing from year to year towards teachers, pupils, students, but also the book lovers themselves, who are none of the above, because we have published more than 300 textbooks in the last few years alone. Today, our house is a leader in textbooks in the Federation of Bosnia and Herzegovina. Authors, editors and professional consultants, showing a high level of knowledge, creativity, and the introduction of new and modern approaches, have made our textbooks the highest quality in the field for which they were written. At the same time, Sarajevo Publishing is a great friend of Bosnian families and an unavoidable partner for schools, libraries, cultural and public institutions. We are a socially responsible company that works every day to improve the existing education system and improve our children.


Sarajevo Publishing has decided to create its own website for online booking, through which book lovers, regardless of whether they are members of the library or not, can book their book on time. By filling out the reservation form, the user of the site leaves his personal data so that he does not have to be a member of the library to pick up his book. The BookStore website is easy to use, which you can see for yourself. Below you will be explained the basic actions of BookStore is a virtual online store. You can view all the books we have on offer, using navigation or search. When you click on the name of the book, you will be able to see all the basic information about it, such as: title, authors, genre to which it belongs, ... Click on the reservation button to enter your personal information to keep records and some security and guarantee. All you have to do is go and get the book you want at the time you want it.Once this bookstore was unique, it is now divided into one central one at a given location and three smaller ones at different location locations around the city. However, each of them can be accessed and a book can be reserved through a given page.



We (Central BookStore) are located at Kulovića 19, Sarajevo 71000 across from the Central Bank of BiH. You can visit us every working day from 08:00 to 20:00 and on Saturdays from 09:00 to 16:00. Come and get your chosen book.
